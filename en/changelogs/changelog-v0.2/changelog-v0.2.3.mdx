* **Updated rule A-8 of leader rules:**
  * **A-8** — "Nepotism Day": Within the first 24 hours from the moment of appointment to the post, the leader has the right to freely distribute any ranks within the faction (appointing deputies, unit heads, etc.).
    * **A-8.1** — During this period, it is allowed to accept an unlimited number of new employees to any positions without official acceptance (**rule** **B-7**).
    * **<span style={{ color: '#ff0000' }}>A-8.2</span>** — <span style={{ color: '#ff0000' }}>For standard dismissal or demotion of a player, a valid legal reason is still required (RP component (</span>**<span style={{ color: '#ff0000' }}>B-7</span>**<span style={{ color: '#ff0000' }}>)).</span>
    * **<span style={{ color: '#ff0000' }}>A-8.3</span>** — <span style={{ color: '#ff0000' }}>On "Nepotism Day," a leader can receive special permission from the Curator of the organization to dismiss employees without explaining legal reasons (e.g., for a complete roster clearance for a new team).</span>
    * **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>A-8.2</span>** — <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>On "Nepotism Day," a leader has the right to freely demote and promote any employees in position without explaining reasons. However, dismissing a player still requires a valid legal reason (RP reason).</span>
    * **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>A-8.3</span>** — <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>A leader can receive special permission from the Curator to dismiss employees without explaining reasons (e.g., for a complete roster clearance).</span>
