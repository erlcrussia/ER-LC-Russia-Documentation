***

* **Added prefixes to the faction table.**
* **Updated rule A-8 of leader rules:**
  * **A-8** — "Nepotism Day": <span style={{ color: '#ff0000' }}>within the first 24 hours of their term, a leader can assign any ranks (e.g., changing unit leaders or deputies).</span> <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>Within the first</span> **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>24 hours</span>** <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>from the moment of appointment to the post, the leader has the right to freely distribute any ranks within the faction (appointing deputies, unit heads, etc.).</span>
    * **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>A-8.1</span>** <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>— During this period, it is allowed to accept an unlimited number of new employees to any positions without official acceptance (rule</span> **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>B-7</span>** <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>).</span>
    * **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>A-8.2</span>** <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>— For standard dismissal or demotion of a player, a valid legal reason is still required (RP component (</span>**<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>B-7</span>**<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>)).</span>
    * **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>A-8.3</span>** <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>— On "Nepotism Day," a leader can receive special</span> **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>permission from the Curator</span>** <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>of the organization to dismiss employees without explaining legal reasons (e.g., for a complete roster clearance for a new team).</span>
* **Updated rule B-6:**
  * **B-6** — <span style={{ color: '#ff0000' }}>Grounds for promotion: successful completion of probation period, positive assessment/exam passing, no disciplinary actions, special merits, Management decision.</span> <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>Promotion is awarded exclusively for justified professional achievements (successful completion of probation period, assessment, exam passing, or special merits confirmed by results); while formal "management decision" is not an independent reason for advancement without a specific employee contribution, and having a ban completely excludes the possibility of promotion.</span>
* **Updated rule B-7:**
  * **B-7** — <span style={{ color: '#ff0000' }}>Grounds for demotion/dismissal: disciplinary violations, systematic non-performance of duties, misconduct, voluntary resignation, Management decision.</span> <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>Any forced demotion or dismissal must have a clear legal game reason, confirmed by evidence.</span>
    * **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>B-7.1</span>** <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>— An employee has the right to leave the faction voluntarily. In this case, dismissal is recorded without the need to provide evidence of violations.</span>
    * **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>B-7.2</span>** <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>— In case of a serious violation of server rules or inadequate behavior by a player, removal from position can be carried out by decision of</span> **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>Management or Curator</span>** <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>of the faction (regardless of the presence of game reasons).</span>
