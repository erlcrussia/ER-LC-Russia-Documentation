From this day forward, order in the city is maintained not only by patrols, but also by the letter of the law. Key supervisory bodies are entering the game:

* **Judicial System** — now any dispute or violation is resolved exclusively within the legal framework.
* **Investigative Committee (IC)** — investigation of the most complex and high-profile criminal cases.
* **Prosecutor's Office** — strict supervision of the legality of actions of all government structures.

### **Reminder:**

These bodies (including FSB) exercise supervision, but **do not command** other factions.

* _Prosecutor's Office and IC_ have the right to conduct inspections of FSB and other factions.
* _FSB's "M" Directorate_ can conduct anti-corruption investigations against prosecutor's office employees and other factions.
* Interference in the internal management of other factions is strictly limited by the rules.

### **Regulation Changes**

In connection with the launch of the Government, we have made critical amendments to the general [**faction rules**](/en/server-rules/fractions-rules/fractions-rules). Be sure to review the updated points!

_Power is not only privileges, but also enormous responsibility._

### Terms of Office for the Government:

Working in the highest supervisory bodies requires stability and deep immersion. In this regard:

* The term of office for leaders (Prosecutor's Office, Investigative Committee, Court) is 45 days (max. 3 consecutive terms).
* We are looking for people ready for long-term development of their departments and creating a quality RP process.

### **Recruitment for Government Factions:**

Within the next few days, applications will be opened for:

* Prosecutor's Office leader position
* Investigative Committee (IC) employees
* Prosecutor's Office and Court employees

**Important:** Selection will take place in two stages: review of written applications and subsequent candidate interviews. We will evaluate not only knowledge of the rules, but also your understanding of the legislative framework.
