        * OPG and MVD.
        * OPG and FSB.

        * <span style={{ color: '#ff0000' }}>OPG and Court.</span>
        * <span style={{ color: '#ff0000' }}>OPG and Prosecutor's Office RF.</span><span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>OPG and Government (IC, Prosecutor's Office, FSO).</span>
        * <span style={{ color: '#ff0000' }}>FSO and OPG.</span>

    * **C-5** — All scheduled <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>and unscheduled</span> inspections by the prosecutor's office must be coordinated in advance with the Fraction Manager.
    * **C-6.2** — If a Management representative personally participated in the process, the decision on its review or cancellation is made exclusively by Senior Management (<span style={{ color: '#ff0000' }}>Owner and Co-Owners</span><span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>Owner and Project Leadership</span>) <span style={{ color: '#ff0000' }}>The verdict of Senior Management is final and not subject to appeal</span><span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>and their decision is not subject to appeal</span>.

    * Sections G-M (MVD, FSB, EMERCOM, MoH, Prosecutor's Office, IC, Court) merged into section **F. Special Part**.
    * **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>F-1 —</span>** <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>The right to conduct inspections of government and private structures belongs to the Prosecutor's Office. Employees of the Mayor's Office are also empowered to carry out inspection activities for businesses.</span>
    * **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>F-2 —</span>** <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>It is not allowed to detain and process a player who has already died in a shootout and ended up in the hospital — a new IC situation is required.</span>
    * **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>F-3 —</span>** <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>Upon official appeal to the FSB (submitting a ticket on the FSB server) and/or initiation of a criminal case, other government employees may be detained or brought to justice by the FSB.</span>
    * **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>F-4 —</span>** <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>The Prosecutor's Office has the right to carry out supervisory activities over government fractions and private organizations.</span>
    * **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>F-5 —</span>** <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>IC employees at the crime scene have special powers and have the right to call MVD employees.</span>
    * **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>F-6 —</span>** <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>FSO employees have the right to organize the work of other departments during security measures.</span>
    * **<span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>F-7 —</span>** <span style={{ color: '#00be00' }}>Fractions have the right to carry out other activities provided for by Russian legislation that do not violate the project rules.</span>
