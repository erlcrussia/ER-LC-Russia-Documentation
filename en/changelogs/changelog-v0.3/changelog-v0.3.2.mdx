<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14"><h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">Referral Link System</h3></div>

<p align="center"><strong>More friends — more opportunities!</strong> We now have a referral link system: send invitations and unlock access to special bonuses and rewards.</p>

<Warning>
  For a referral link to work, at least **3 months** must have passed since the user left the server. This period is not final and may be changed.
</Warning>

***

<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14"><h4 className="text-2xl font-bold">New Commands</h4></div>

* **`/create link`**
  * Creates your referral link.
* **`/my link`**
  * Shows information about your referral link.
  * **Displayed information:**
    * Referral link.
    * Number of invited users.
    * Position in the ranking.
    * Button to view the list of invited users.

<Info>
  At the moment, self-deletion of referral links by users is not provided. If you need to delete a referral link, please create a ticket with a valid reason for this action.
</Info>

      * **F-9.1** — Situation rollback is allowed only with the consent of all participants in the RP scene or game chat. If at least one participant disagrees — rollback is prohibited. Exception: staff decision.
      * **F-9.2** — In case of a car accident or chase, failure to roleplay the crash without a rollback is considered a violation.
      * **F-9.3** — If three or more people are involved in a chase and a collision occurs, rollback is prohibited — all parties must roleplay the consequences.
      * **F-9.4** — If two players are involved in a chase and both parties continue moving after an accident, both receive punishment, regardless of who caused the collision.
      * **F-9.5** — An accidental car accident can be rolled back according to general rules — only with the consent of all participants.
      * **F-9.6** — If a car accident was caused by one player's fault (e.g., hitting a pole or colliding with a wall), it is necessary to write or say the message ".// rollback" in the "game-chat" or RP scene.
