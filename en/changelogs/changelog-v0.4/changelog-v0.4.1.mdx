<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14"><h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">Forum</h3></div>

* Added the ability to create guides — publish step-by-step instructions on any topic.
  * **Important:** only original, useful, and visual content (screenshots/videos are mandatory).
* Added "Record Book" — document your achievements. Requires evidence, honest execution, and transparent results.
* Added "Off-Topic" section — free communication on any topic with respect and no spam.

<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14"><h3 className="text-3xl font-bold"><strong>RP Scene Relocation and ProxiLink Update</strong></h3></div>

* We are moving RP scenes to the [**Civilian Operations**](https://discord.gg/dpvA2zxK4u) server and disabling the Emergency Services bot. The new server will provide more interaction opportunities and add atmosphere to RP.
* You don't need to leave the old server — something interesting will appear there soon)
* **ProxiLink has also been updated:**
  * Improved location detection system.
  * Added transfer to the waiting channel if the player is not near you.

***

<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14"><h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">New Faction: FSO</h3></div>

* Added a new government faction — the Federal Protective Service (FSO). The main task of the unit is to ensure the complete security of the highest state officials. FSO is the main state protection agency in Russia.
* The faction is part of the Government and interacts with other law enforcement agencies.
