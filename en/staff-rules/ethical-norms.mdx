<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-10">
  <h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">A. General Provisions</h3>
</div>

* **A-1 —** The Code is a set of fundamental principles and ethical norms that define the moral and ethical guidelines of activity. It is declarative and educational in nature, formulating ideology, values, and corporate culture. Its task is to establish general rules of behavior based on the concepts of honor, dignity, and professional ethics.
* **A-2 —** This code is mandatory for all Staff employees of the Moscow RolePlay server, including rules of interaction with players.
* **A-3 —** Guided by the rules of the Moscow RolePlay server, principles of equality, and common sense, this regulation defines the role of Staff as an element of the judicial system and a guarantor of law and order. Given the special nature of the relationship between Staff and players, as well as the need to regulate these relations, norms of Administrative Ethics are introduced.
* **A-4 —** The regulation proclaims that each Staff employee bears personal responsibility to Senior Staff for their actions and decisions. In turn, Senior Staff bears responsibility for their employees before the entire gaming community.

***

<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14">
  <h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">B. Professional Standards and Obligations</h3>
</div>

* **B-1 —** Staff must be ready to assist any player, without discrimination on any grounds, including gender, age, race, nationality, social status, religious or political beliefs, as well as financial situation.
* **B-2 —** Staff must know all or the vast majority of the server rules on which they work.
* **B-3 —** Staff must in every possible way contribute to improving the quality of role-play (RP), including conducting educational activities on RP, rules, etc.
* **B-4 —** Staff are prohibited from ignoring or leaving without response any cases of cruelty, toxic behavior, or humiliation of human dignity.
* **B-5 —** The main condition of Staff activity is professional competence, their special knowledge, and the art of judgment.
* **B-6 —** It is precisely professional competence, along with a humanistic moral position that assumes high demands on oneself, the ability to recognize and correct one's own mistakes, that gives a Staff employee the right to make independent decisions.
* **B-7 —** Staff are required to monitor the quality of their work.
* **B-8 —** Staff must make every effort to ensure that the quality of their work is at a high level.

***

<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14">
  <h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">C. Code of Ethics, Prohibitions, and Rights</h3>
</div>

* **C-1 —** Causing harm to a player in any form is prohibited: physical, material, or moral, both intentionally and through negligence.
* **C-2 —** Staff are required to prevent such actions not only from their side but also from other persons.
* **C-3 —** Using official knowledge and powers for personal purposes is incompatible with the status of Staff.
* **C-4 —** Staff are prohibited from using their capabilities for inhumane purposes, applying measures without sufficient grounds, or refusing them, using powers to harm a player or in the interests of third parties.
* **C-5 —** It is prohibited to impose personal philosophical, religious, or political views on players. Personal prejudices and other unprofessional motives must not influence decisions made.
* **C-6 —** Staff must not mislead players with their actions.
* **C-7 —** Staff are prohibited from using player labor for personal purposes, as well as engaging in extortion. However, Staff have the right to accept gratitude from players and their relatives.
* **C-8 —** Staff are required to cease cooperation with any person if they demand actions that violate server rules, professional duty, or ethical norms.
* **C-9 —** Staff have the right to refuse to work with players in the following cases:
  * If they feel insufficiently competent, or do not have the necessary technical capabilities to provide assistance.
  * If Staff feel unwell, or in case of technical problems.
* **C-10 —** All Staff employees are strictly prohibited from disclosing internal information, correspondence, Staff decisions, and technical details (NDA).

***

<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14">
  <h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">D. Staff and Player Rights</h3>
</div>

* **D-1 —** Staff and the right of players to freedom and independence of personality.
* **D-2 —** Staff must respect the honor and dignity of the player. Rough and inhumane treatment of a player, humiliation of their human dignity, as well as any manifestations of superiority or expression of preference or dislike for any players by Staff are unacceptable.
* **D-3 —** Staff must provide assistance with minimal possible restriction of player freedom, and when establishing control over player behavior, strictly limit interference within the framework of server rules.
* **D-4 —** Staff must behave appropriately towards players, as well as towards higher-ranking staff.

***

<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14">
  <h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">E. Staff and Colleagues</h3>
</div>

* **E-1** **—** In relation to colleagues, a Staff employee must behave as they would like them to behave towards them.
* **E-2 —** In relationships with Staff colleagues, an employee must be honest, fair, benevolent, decent, must respect their knowledge and experience, and must be ready to selflessly share their experience and knowledge.
* **E-3 —** Criticism of a colleague must be well-founded and non-offensive. Professional actions are subject to criticism, not the personality of colleagues.
* **E-4 —** Attempts to strengthen one's own authority by discrediting colleagues are unacceptable. Staff must not allow negative statements about their colleagues and their work in the presence of players.

***

<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14">
  <h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">F. Limits of the Code of Ethics</h3>
</div>

* **F-1 —** Responsibility for violation of professional ethics is determined by Senior Staff.
* **F-2 —** The first judge of a Staff employee is their conscience. The second is Senior Staff, which has the right to impose a penalty on the violator in accordance with the rules and other documents.

***

<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14">
  <h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">G. Staff Oath</h3>
</div>

I, having embarked on the path of fighting violators and maintaining order on the server, a Staff employee, pledge and swear to perform my duties conscientiously, without selfish motives, and strictly within the framework of the server and Staff rules.

I am the face of the server. It is I who must set an example for other players: how to behave on the server, how to communicate with each other, how to play your role and understand ERLC.\
It is I who represent the interests and rights of players. I act as their judge, prosecutor, and lawyer simultaneously.

A ban is an extreme measure of punishment, created for the most hardened players. It is created to TEACH players who don't listen, not to take revenge on them for hurting your feelings. Try to use verbal warnings to avoid being accused of intentionally harming the server by depriving it of adequate players.\
Minimum disputes — maximum gameplay. Many of you claim that you are in staff to help the server, and even if that is true, you will help the most if you PLAY, rather than controlling everything and everyone. Allow players to sometimes break rules if most people enjoy it and it adds fun.\
Be merciful. Some players break rules. Often this is due to some catalysts, like a bad day. If a person realizes their guilt — give them another chance. Within reason.
