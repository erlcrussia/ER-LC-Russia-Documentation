<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14"><h3 className="text-3xl font-bold"><strong>How to Join the Server?</strong></h3></div>

**To join our server, you will need to:**

1. Enter the game and click on the three lines in the top right corner

<Frame>
  <img src="/assets/guides/step-one.png" alt="Step 1" />
</Frame>

2. Go to the Servers tab and make sure you have one hour of playtime in the main game, as well as 500 XP in any of the in-game factions (police, firefighters, sheriff, or DOT). You don't need to earn 500 XP in each one — having 500 XP in just one of them is enough. You can view this information in the Players tab by clicking on your character.

  <img src="/assets/guides/step-two.png" alt="Step 2" />

<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-16"><h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">What Should I Do in the Early Stages?</h3></div>

1. **First, you need to properly set up your game character:**
    * Your character is the main object of roleplay, so it's important to get it in proper shape. The body must be strictly classic (square, R15, without rounded parts). The skin should not have items that a person wouldn't wear in everyday life: wings, horns, items with effects, pets on shoulders, swords, non-standard animations, or unnatural skin tone. The appearance should not stand out from the bounds of everyday reality.
    * If you're playing as part of a faction (police, firefighters, road services) or decided to open your own business, having a nice uniform corresponding to your rank and specialty is only welcome!
