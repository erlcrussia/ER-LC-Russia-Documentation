* **Guide** — a step-by-step instruction, action plan, or manual. Our users can create them not only on game mechanics, web resource operations, or questions related to our team, but also on any other topics.
* **For a guide to be as useful as possible and get approved, it must meet certain requirements:**
  * A guide is meant to teach the user something new. There's no room for discussing unrelated topics, philosophical musings, or unnecessary information. Write clearly and to the point.
  * The text must be fully original. If you use third-party sources, be sure to include links to them.
  * The entire process should be accompanied by visual images. If necessary, use video or GIF animations for better understanding of the material.

* **Record Book** — the official registry of outstanding achievements of our players. It records results that go beyond normal gameplay and demonstrate exceptional skill, perseverance, or creativity.
* **For your application to be considered and added to the list of records, it must meet the following criteria:**
  * Evidence and Accuracy A record is a fact confirmed by numbers. The application should not contain unnecessary descriptions or artistic digressions.
  * Clean Execution The achievement must be obtained fairly. Using bugs or assistance from administration (unless provided for by the record's conditions) will invalidate the application. When using borrowed ideas or methods, be sure to cite the original source.
  * Visual Confirmation Any record must be documented. High-quality screenshots are accepted (without cropping or blurred elements), video recordings, or links to logs (if applicable). The entire process of setting the record must be transparent and clear.

* **Off-Topic** — a section for communication on free topics that don't fit into the main forum categories. Here our users can take a break from game mechanics, web resource operations, and official matters to simply chat.
* **To keep the section interesting and free of clutter, follow these rules:**
  * No topic restrictions Here you can discuss everything: from new movies and music to personal stories and hobbies. Write about what interests you, share your thoughts, and find like-minded people.
  * Respect for the community Freedom of communication doesn't cancel politeness. Insults, toxic behavior, and provocations are prohibited. Off-topic is created for relaxation, not for settling disputes.
  * Original content and finds If you create something of your own or found cool content online — feel free to share. When using third-party resources, it's desirable to credit the author or source.
  * Meaningful content Despite this being a section "for everything," try to avoid meaningless spam. Messages consisting of random characters or carrying no message may be deleted to avoid cluttering the feed.
