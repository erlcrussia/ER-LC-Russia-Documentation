* **A-1** — At regular events (NOT RP), failure to follow staff instructions is prohibited, such as ignoring commands from the host/staff, as well as leaving the event area without permission.
  * **A-1.1** — Ignoring the host's commands in the Discord scene is also considered a violation.
* **A-2** — At regular events (NOT RP), creating interference with the event is prohibited: running around, ramming, honking, and other noisy actions that disturb participants, as well as interfering in contests/challenges/mini-games if you are not a participant.
  * **A-2.1** — **Discord-specific:** It is prohibited to talk over the host, play music into the microphone, or make sharp sounds that prevent participants from hearing the contest rules. Interfering in challenges if you are not a participant is prohibited.
* **A-3** — At regular events (NOT RP), incorrect use of vehicles is prohibited: using vehicles prohibited for the event, blocking roads, or obstructing participant routes.
* **A-4** — At regular events (NOT RP), unauthorized use of special abilities is prohibited: using faction functions (handcuffs, arrest, spikes, etc.) without organizer instruction, as well as using PD/SD/EMS/FD/DOT equipment outside the scenario.
  * **A-4.1** — Ignoring the host's commands in the Discord scene is also considered a violation.
* **A-5** — At regular events (NOT RP), using weapons without the host's permission is prohibited, including the use of heavy weaponry if it is not part of the scenario.
  * **A-5.1** — Ignoring the host's commands in the Discord scene is also considered a violation.
* **A-6** — At regular events (NOT RP), violating the queue and organizational discipline is prohibited: entering the game area before being called, as well as any actions that interfere with the normal conduct of a contest or task.
  * **A-6.1** — Ignoring the host's commands in the Discord scene is also considered a violation.
* **A-7** — At regular events (NOT RP), noise and chaotic actions are prohibited: excessive use of signals/sirens/music/effects, as well as creating chaos (accidents, mass fights) if it is not part of the scenario.
  * **A-7.1** — Ignoring the host's commands in the Discord scene is also considered a violation.

_**The moderator decides what punishment to give for a violation. The punishment depends on how serious the violation was.**_
