<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-10">
  <h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">A. General Rules</h3>
</div>

- **A-1** — These rules are mandatory for all fractions and supplement the main rules, server rules, and other project documentation.
- **A-2** — All fractions are equal to each other and are subordinate to Management as the highest governing body of the project.
- **A-3** — Any changes to the structure of fractions (creation/closure of departments, introduction of new positions, changes to the hierarchy) are subject to mandatory coordination with Project Management before they come into effect.
- **A-4** — ERLC game ranks (XP level) are system-based and are not related to internal fraction ranks. Fraction leadership is not responsible for the availability of in-game items. If certain equipment is unavailable to you, you need to accumulate game experience (XP) on public servers on your own. Your actual rank in the fraction is determined exclusively by your role on the Discord server.
- **A-5** — **Status of Government Employees:**
  - **A-5.1** — Employees of government agencies (MVD, FSB, EMERCOM, Ministry of Health) are representatives of authority and are required to act strictly within the framework of the Constitution of the Russian Federation and federal laws (including the Criminal Code of the Russian Federation, the Code of Administrative Offenses of the Russian Federation, the Federal Law "On Police", the Federal Law "On Security", the Federal Law "On Fire Safety", the Federal Law "On Fundamentals of Protecting the Health of Citizens").
    <Callout icon="pen-to-square" iconType="regular" color="#E0B700">
      **_Note:_** _This provision is explanatory in nature and shall not be used as grounds for holding liable._
    </Callout>
  - **A-5.2** — Any official activity must have an RP justification and correspond to the nature of the real work of the respective agency, without absurd or knowingly unlawful actions.
- **A-6** — **Criminal Activity of Government Employees:**
  - **A-6.1** — Employees of government agencies are prohibited from any criminal actions without a pre-written and approved RP situation describing infiltration, corruption, etc.
    <Callout icon="circle-info" iconType="regular" color="#b10000">
      **_Exception —_** _pre-approved undercover special operations._
    </Callout>
  - **A-6.2** — Senior management (ministers, their deputies, heads of main departments/directorates, their deputies) are prohibited from having criminal activity, including receiving bribes; the exception is pre-approved undercover special operations.
  - **A-6.3** — It is prohibited to use official functionality (databases, wanted lists, calls, special signals, etc.) for personal purposes, trolling, or pressuring players.
- **A-7** — All fraction employees are required to record all role-play scenes on video, especially in cases where the situation may cause controversial or conflicting interpretations. The absence of a video recording during an investigation may be considered a violation.
- **A-8** — **Appearance and Behavior:**
  - **A-8.1** — Government agency employees must comply with the departmental dress code: service uniform, neat appearance, no absurd accessories (rule F-3 [of the main server rules](/en/server-rules/general-rules/server-rules)).
    <Callout icon="pen-to-square" iconType="regular" color="#E0B700">
      **_Note:_** _The use of faction accessories from a faction you are not a member of may be considered a violation._
    </Callout>
  - **A-8.2** — In functional "green zones" (agency buildings, government facilities), behavior inconsistent with the status of a government employee is prohibited: informal jokes, rudeness, bribe-taking, drunkenness, etc., Non RP behavior in agency buildings; being inside agency buildings does not exempt players from complying with the general project rules.
- **A-9** — An employee is prohibited from being promoted by more than one sequential step, as well as any promotion more frequently than once every 24 hours. Violation of these terms and procedures is punishable by a reprimand to the leadership and annulment of the employee's promotion.
  - **Exception:** Violation of the established terms or promotion procedures is allowed only with the permission of the **Curator / Chief Curator / Fraction Manager**.
  - **Restriction after annulment:** If an employee was demoted by a curator due to a violation of promotion rules, their next legitimate promotion is possible strictly 24 hours after that demotion.

  > **Example:** Promoting an employee twice in one day or immediately by several ranks without approval is prohibited. If a curator annuls such a promotion, the timer for the next appointment resets to 24 hours.

- **B-2** — Recruitment is carried out strictly through official procedures: applications, interviews, exams, or testing established by the fraction documentation.
  - **B-2.1** — Recruitment into the Government (IC, Prosecutor's Office, Court, and others) is carried out strictly in coordination with Management. The Fraction Manager or their deputies usually conduct the review, but in certain cases, another Management representative may do so.
  - **B-2.2** — Minimum requirements for a candidate: a Discord account, compliance with the main rules, no serious active penalties, compliance with age criteria and fraction requirements.
- **B-3** — Probation period: may be established for new employees (recommended duration — up to 14 days). During the probation period, dismissal without explanation is possible in case of low activity or violations.
- **B-4** — "Buddy" recruitment bypassing the procedure is prohibited; such appointments are deemed illegitimate and annulled.
- **B-5** — Promotion is granted exclusively for justified professional achievements (successful completion of probation, certification, passing exams, or special merits confirmed by results); a formal "management decision" is not an independent reason for advancement without a specific contribution from the employee, and having a ban completely excludes the possibility of promotion.
- **B-6** — Any forced demotion or dismissal must have a clear legitimate in-game reason, confirmed by evidence.
  - **B-6.1** — An employee has the right to leave the fraction of their own free will. In this case, dismissal is recorded without the need to provide evidence of violations.
  - **B-6.2** — In case of a gross violation of server rules by a player or inadequate behavior, dismissal from the position may be carried out by decision of **Management or the Curator** of the fraction (regardless of the presence of in-game reasons).
- **B-7** — **Personnel Decision Procedure:**
  - **B-7.1** — Senior staff are appointed by the fraction leader with mandatory notification of Management.
  - **B-7.2** — Demotion for a violation must be accompanied by a reference to the basis; the employee is given the opportunity to provide explanations, except in cases of gross violation requiring immediate dismissal.
- **B-8** — All personnel changes are recorded through the "Department Management MRP" bot commands.
- **B-9** — Multi-fraction and position combination.
  - **B-9.1 —** A player is required to fulfill the full volume of established quotas (shift/report norms) in each of their chosen fractions. Failure to meet the norm in one fraction due to being busy in another is not an excuse and is punished in accordance with the internal charter.

<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14">
  <h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">C. Fraction Activities and Events</h3>
</div>

- **C-1** — It is prohibited to: artificially prolong RP for statistics, refuse RP without a valid reason, abuse authority, use a position for personal gain.
- **C-2** — Mass events (formations, drills, etc.) involving the gathering of employees require coordination with Fraction Management at least 24 hours in advance; the application must contain the purpose, time, location, approximate number of participants, and scenario.
  <Callout icon="pen-to-square" iconType="regular" color="#E0B700">
    **_Note:_** _Uncoordinated mass events entail disciplinary responsibility, up to the removal of leadership._
  </Callout>
- **C-4** — When responding to a call, the following conditions must be observed:
  - **C-4.1** — The rule applies when there are **at least 6 online fraction employees**.
  - **C-4.2** — At least 50% of online fraction employees must remain unresponsive to the call to ensure a reserve.
  - **C-4.3** — If employees who arrived at the call cannot cope, calling for backup is allowed.
  - **C-4.4** — All online employees can respond to the same call only with management approval (in cases where a large number of people are required in one location, for example, during events).
- **C-5** — All scheduled and unscheduled inspections by the prosecutor's office must be coordinated in advance with the Fraction Manager.
- **C-6** — **Management Participation in the RP Process**
  - **C-6.1** — Management representatives who did not personally participate in a specific trial, investigation, or inspection have the right to annul any decisions made or their results if violations are identified.
  - **C-6.2** — If a Management representative personally participated in the process, the decision on its review or cancellation is made exclusively by Senior Management (Owner and Project Leadership) and their decision is not subject to appeal.

<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14">
  <h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">D. Interaction with Other Fractions</h3>
</div>

- **D-1** — Interaction must be respectful and constructive, based on the interests of the gameplay.
- **D-2** — Provocations between fractions, systematic ignoring of justified requests, and creating "hostile" relationships are prohibited.
- **D-3** — Interaction with civilians: responding to justified requests (calls, assistance) is mandatory, correct RP communication and explaining one's actions, using force without a clear RP reason is prohibited.
- **D-4** — Interaction with non-governmental fractions (media, advocacy, private security, etc.): recognize legitimate RP status, do not obstruct their professional activities without RP grounds.
- **D-5** — All alliances and agreements with other fractions must be documented in writing and brought to the attention of Management.
- **D-6 —** Leaders and fraction members are strictly prohibited from initiating inter-fraction wars and provoking large-scale conflicts. Curators are required to strictly suppress any attempts to start a war, acting as guarantors of diplomacy and order on the server.

***

<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14">
  <h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">E. Discipline, Behavior, and Sanctions</h3>
</div>

- **E-1** — Fraction members are required to comply with the main rules, Discord rules, and RP rules, maintain a business communication style, wear uniforms, and use RP nicknames.
- **E-2** — Toxicity, insults, threats, harassment, abuse of authority, and disclosure of confidential fraction information to third parties without official necessity are prohibited.
- **E-3** — Leadership is required to respond promptly to violations, record incidents, and conduct internal investigations; concealing violations is equated to committing them.
- **E-4** — Systematic violations or gross misconduct may result in a warning, demotion, suspension, or dismissal (up to disciplinary measures by Fraction Management).

***

<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14">
  <h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">F. Special Part</h3>
</div>

- **F-1 —** The right to conduct inspections of the activities of government and private structures belongs to the Prosecutor's Office. In relation to private organizations (businesses), employees of the Mayor's Office are also empowered to carry out inspection activities.
- **F-2 —** It is not allowed to detain and process a player who has already died in a shootout and ended up in the hospital — a new IC situation is required.
- **F-3 —** Upon official appeal to the FSB (submitting a ticket on the FSB server) and/or initiation of a criminal case, other employees of government structures of the Russian Federation may be detained or brought to justice by the FSB, with the participation of investigators and further court and legal proceedings and/or application of sanctions with the help of the FSB Curator and FSB Director.
- **F-4 —** The Prosecutor's Office of the Russian Federation has the right to carry out supervisory activities in relation to government fractions and private organizations. The department has the full range of powers to control the activities of organizations (both internal and external).
- **F-5 —** IC employees at the crime scene have special powers and have the right to call MVD employees. Criminal Investigation employees working on the case are transferred under the supervision of the IC investigator to carry out operational search activities, capture, and deliver the suspect for interrogation.
- **F-6 —** FSO employees have the right to organize the work of other departments when conducting security measures for an object. FSO employees may also restrict access of any persons to the protected territory, if necessary, prevent their penetration or detain them, and then deliver them to the UMVD.
- **F-7 —** Fractions have the right to carry out other activities provided for by the legislation of the Russian Federation, but not violating the rules of the project.
