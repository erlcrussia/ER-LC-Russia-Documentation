<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-10">
  <h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">Section A — General Provisions</h3>
</div>

* **A-1** — All OCGs are subject to the main rules of the Moscow Rp server regardless of the situation. Official OCG status does not grant any leniency for violating them.
* **A-2** — If you want to log in as your OCG character and roleplay as them, you must set a prefix at the DMV building near the spawn.
  * If you do not have a prefix, it is considered that your character is not a member of this OCG, and you have no right to cooperate in any way with the OCG you are a part of.
    <Tip>
      **Example prefix:** Triada I Alexander
    </Tip>
  * The first word must be the name of your OCG, and the second must be either your callsign in the OCG or any other text.

* **A-3** — An unofficial OCG is considered to be any group of three or more people, having its own form (uniform, insignia) and carrying weapons, as well as committing any illegal actions. The existence of such groups violates the rules.
    <Note>
      **Unofficial OCGs also include:** any PMCs and unofficial security organizations.
    </Note>
* **A-4** — Dress code. Each official OCG must have an established clothing style or distinguishing color (e.g., at least 50% of clothing must be green). It is forbidden to commit raids in clothing that does not match the organization's declared style.
* **A-5** — Territory capture. Official OCGs have the right to initiate capture of territories and objects to expand their influence.
  * **A-5.1** — Announcement regulations: A capture is considered valid only after posting a message in a special Discord channel using the established template. (Discord channel: `#🔫┃захват-территорий`).
  * **A-5.2** — Mandatory ping: When submitting a capture request, the attacking OCG must ping the leader of the defending side and 4 of their online members.
  * **A-5.3** — During a shootout, rescue services are prohibited from reviving a person in order to continue the shootout.
  * **A-5.4** — Restrictions: It is forbidden to capture or conduct combat operations on the territories of State Institutions (Green zones: police, hospitals, courts) and on the spawn territories of other OCGs.
    <Danger>
      **Important clarification on zones:** River City — territory capture **PROHIBITED**. Springfield — territory capture **ALLOWED**.
    </Danger>

***

<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14">
  <h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">Section B — Duties and Activity</h3>
</div>

* **B-1** — Activity. An official OCG is obliged to maintain the status of an active group.
  * **B-1.1** — Weekly norm: The leader or their deputies must conduct at least 3 significant RP situations per week (robberies, kidnappings, terrorist acts).
  * **B-1.2** — Evening online: OCG members must be active between 17:00 and 22:00 MSK to maintain crime activity on the server.
  * **B-1.3** — Inactivity: If an OCG shows no activity for 5 days without prior notification to the Civil Operations Manager, the OCG may receive a warning/reprimand.

***

<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14">
  <h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">Section C — Prohibitions and Restrictions</h3>
</div>

* **C-1** — It is forbidden to have more than five people from one OCG online on the server at the same time. Any cooperation with an OCG without a set prefix, aimed at obtaining benefits for it, will be considered a circumvention of this rule and will be punished.
* **C-2** — Any negative statements towards any OCG, including on various social networks, are prohibited.
* **C-3** — Crime cooldown. After completing a major crime (bank robbery or hostage taking), an OCG is forbidden from committing similar actions for 20 minutes. For petty crime (robbing a store register/player), a cooldown of 5 minutes is set.
* **C-4** — Shootouts, robberies, murders, and break-ins can only be committed when there are at least 2 police officers on the police team.
  * Kidnapping or taking hostages is only allowed for a limited time. For example, during a kidnapping, to obtain a ransom from the victim's associates.
  * When taking hostages, you must put forward reasonable and serious demands that the police can fulfill to avoid unnecessary casualties.
  * Killing hostages is only allowed in the most hopeless situation, or when the police do not make contact and do not fulfill the demands.
* **C-4.1** — Robberies of large businesses can only be committed when there are 4 police officers in the game. If at the start of the criminal situation there were enough police officers, and during the process their number decreased for any reason, the situation can still be continued.
    <Tip>
      **Note:** Large businesses are considered to be the bank and the jewelry store.
    </Tip>

***

<div className="flex justify-center items-center w-full -mt-14">
  <h3 className="text-3xl font-bold">Section D — Disciplinary Regulations</h3>
</div>

* **D-1** — For violating rules, the Civil Operations Manager has the right to apply the following sanctions:
  * Give a verbal warning.
  * Issue a warning.
  * Issue a reprimand.
* **D-2** — Maximum number of reprimands and warnings: 3. Upon reaching the maximum number of reprimands and warnings, the following sanctions may be applied to the OCG:
  * Dissolution of the entire OCG.
  * Issuance of a game ban to the OCG's leadership.
* **D-3** — Removal of reprimands:
  1. Reprimands are removed after two weeks. If the OCG violates a rule during this period, the reprimand removal counter resets.
  2. Warnings are removed after one week, only if the OCG has no reprimands. If the OCG violates a rule during the warning removal period, the counter resets.
    <Note>
      **Note:** Please note that there are **no** special types of punishments for OCGs other than general reprimands and warnings. In the event of a violation by an OCG, players who are members of that OCG may receive regular warns, just like regular server players.
    </Note>
